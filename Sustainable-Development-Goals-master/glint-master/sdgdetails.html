Overview of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

SDG 1: No Poverty

Aims to end poverty in all its forms everywhere by addressing its causes, impacts, and implementing social protection systems to reduce vulnerability.

SDG 2: Zero Hunger

Focuses on achieving food security and improved nutrition through sustainable agriculture practices, striving to end hunger and malnutrition.

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being

Promotes healthy lives for all ages, emphasizing universal health coverage, maternal health, and the fight against diseases.

SDG 4: Quality Education

Advocates for inclusive and equitable quality education, promoting lifelong learning opportunities and enhancing access for marginalized groups.
